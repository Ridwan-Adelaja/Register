Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy quick dish, perfect for any meal. The classic Omelette combines
beaten eggs cooked to perfect, optional filled with your choice of cheese,
vegetable, or meats.

Preperation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preperation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to teste
  • Pepper, to test
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herds

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the the eggs with a pinch of salt and Pepper until they they are
    will mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: placea non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: once the butter is melted ajnd bubbling, pour in the eggs.Tile the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begins to set at the edge but are stillslightly
    renny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Folder and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the filling. Letit cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed
